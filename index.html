What is Sweet Citrus Designs?

Hi! My name is Melissa Figueroa, and Sweet Citrus Designs is the online home showcasing some of what I create. What do I make here? Websites and designs, of course! Over the past couple years, I have been growing my experience and expanding my capabilities.

Proficient Skills

  • HTML and CSS
  • Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Express, and Lightroom
  • Krita
  • Website creation
  • Logo and branding creation
  • Social media content creation
  • Instagram management

Emerging Skills

  • React, Angular, and Gatsby JS
  • C#, SQL, JavaScript, and PHP
  • Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and InDesign
  • Affinity Designer
  • Canva
  • ProPresenter
  • Photography
  • Twitter management

As a front-end web developer, I aim to put God first in everything I do; that means using my skills with humility to help those around me in the best ways that I can. I think that little details in design can make a big difference, whether it be through accessibility features or charming easter eggs that make using the web enjoyable. I aspire to make charming designs that benefit people.
