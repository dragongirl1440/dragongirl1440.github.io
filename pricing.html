Pricing and Details

Thank you for taking an interest in what I do. If you would like to reach out to me for commissions or otherwise, here are some guidelines and price-points to keep in mind:

Details and Guidelines

To request any deliverables, the following must be agreed to:

  • No deliverables will be used in any AI training, or used to supply material for generative AI. This includes all assets I have personally created.
  • Credit will be given.
  • 30% of payment will be received upfront. The remaining 70% will be paid once the deliverables are ready to be sent.
  • Cancellation is OK, but if deliverables were 75% complete or more at the point of cancellation, the 30% upfront fee will not be refunded.
  • When sending a design request, include the word "orange" somewhere in the message body to indicate the guidelines have been read.
  • If I am hired for freelance work, I reserve the right to decline or back out of any project request by notifying beforehand. If I decline or back out, a full refund will be given.

Pricing

Note: Prices are baseline and may vary slightly depending on project details, number of pages, programming languages used, etc.

  • Pure HTML, JS & CSS | $35 USD per page
  • HTML, JS, CSS, & Bootstrap | $20 USD per page
  • Simple React Web App | $100 USD per page
  • Website Maintenance and Upkeep | $10 USD per page
  • Logo Design | $35 USD per icon set of color, black, & white
  • Social Media Post Design | $10 USD per post, multiple aspect ratios
  • Other Opportunities | Email me and let's discuss!

If you would like to discuss opportunities or receive a free quote, please do not hesitate to reach out!
